All math is based on the material assumed to be standard steel

Energy formula (for a ring pair):  
E = ½ · m_total · r² · ω²
- **ring-style rotors**
- **radius = 0.15 m**
- **mass listed = total for both wheels**

## Stored energy (kJ)
_(If your wheels are more “solid disk” than “ring”, roughly halve these.)_

100 kJ is roughly equivalent to:
- lifting **75 kg** about **135 m**
- running a **500 W tool** for about **3 minutes**
- about **25–50 strong grapple pulls** depending on gearing and losses
