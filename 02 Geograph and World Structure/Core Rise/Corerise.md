# Names
Corerise / Radiant Pole Drylands

## Location
The region surrounding the permanently bright polar point.

## Wind Behavior
Slow dry descending airflow.

## Characteristics

- Extremely strong core radiation do to the lack of the Primary shadowbelts coverage
- Very sparse cloud cover
- Weak horizontal winds
- Slow dry descending airflow

## Climate

Air reaching this region has already lost most of its moisture in the surrounding storm crown.  
As a result, the Corerise tends to be comparatively dry.

## Consequences

- sparse ecosystems
- harsh wind exposure
- limited settlement
- high visibility skies

This region marks the geographic center of the global wind system.
