Periodic events where atmospheric drift lanes bring large numbers of islands into close proximity.  
  
These events are not part of natural climate seasons but can create major cultural and economic cycles.  
  
Possible effects  
- temporary trade megacorridors  
- migration opportunities  
- conflict over newly reachable territory  
- rapid species dispersal between ecosystems  
  
These events may occur on multi-year drift cycles and could become major calendar markers for civilizations.
