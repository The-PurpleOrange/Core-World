## 00_SYSTEM_CORE (Locked Physics)

These are “laws of reality.”

Create notes:

- Core Light Source
- Shadow Belts
- Atmospheric Circulation
- Cloud Sea Mechanics
- Lunar Influence
- Drift Mechanics
- Environmental Design Laws

You almost never change these once stable.

---

## 01_WORLD_STRUCTURE

Physical layout of the world.

Notes:

- Island Classification
- Anchored Islands
- Belt Islands
- Cluster Islands
- Drifters
- Island Spacing
- Contact Frequency
- Sky Weather Lanes
- Mapping Methods

Each island type gets its own file.

---

## 02_TIME_AND_CYCLES

Keep time separate from geography.

Notes:

- The Day (Shadow Cycle)
- The Turning
- The Six Phases
- The Great Cycle
- Seasonal Variability
- Date Formatting Standards
- Cultural Timekeeping Differences

This makes calendar logic easy to adjust later.

---

## 03_POWER_AND_TECH

All environmental mechanics go here.

Notes:

- Global Forces Overview
- Thermal Updraft Systems
- Belt Wind Drives
- Tether Differential Engines
- Pressure Wells
- Cloud Gravity Engines
- Gravity Storage Networks
- Regional Tech Distribution

Keep tech separated from physics — tech uses physics but isn’t physics.

## 04_ECOLOGY

Notes:

- Surface Biomes
- Lightbelt Agriculture
- Fog Water Cycle
- Underside Biome

- Pollination Symbiosis
- Storm Survival Strategies

This prevents ecology from getting mixed into physics files.

## 05_CIVILIZATION

This is the most flexible folder.

Notes like:

- Anchored Industrial States
- Belt Trade Cities
- Cluster Villages
- Storm Season Economies
- Engineering Guilds
- Cultural Curses and Language
- Seasonal Warfare
- Migration Cycles

This is where world flavor grows.

---

## 06_STORY

Keep this cleanly separate from lore.

Subfolders:

Characters  
Plot Arcs  
Themes  
POV Notes  
Locations (Narrative Version)  
Myths and Beliefs

Very important:  
Story notes should reference world notes using Obsidian links like:

[[Belt Wind Drives]]  
[[Stormrise Phase]]  
[[Anchored Islands]]

Don’t copy lore into story notes. Link it.

---

## 07_REFERENCE

Quick-access condensed sheets.

Create:

- WORLD SYSTEM SUMMARY (1 page)
- Timeline Quick Chart
- Drift Speed Table
- Season Length Table
- Energy Comparison Table

This is your “don’t dig through folders” area.
